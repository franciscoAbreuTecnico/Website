The TLM01i is a combustion motorcycle equipped with a 250cc, 4-stroke engine from Sherco. Structurally, the bike features a twin spar frame made of trellised aluminum. In 2014, the motorcycle weighed approximately 105kg and boasted a top speed of around 170km/h. Since its debut in the Motostudent 2014 competition, the TLM01i has undergone several modifications to enhance its performance.
