MOTOSTUDENT 2018

The team made its debut in the electric category in this edition with the TLM02e prototype. In the phase where teams are evaluated as business projects (MS1), the team secured the 21st position. However, some issues were encountered with the prototype in the second phase (MS2) and unfortunately the team was unable to complete the proposed tests. Overall, TLMoto concluded this edition in the 22nd position.
