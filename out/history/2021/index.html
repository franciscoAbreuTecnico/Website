MOTOSTUDENT 2021

In 2021, the team competed with the third prototype, the TLM03e. This competition consists of two phases: MS1, where teams are evaluated as a business project, and TLMoto secured the 7th position. In MS2, which focuses on the evaluation of the prototype through a series of tests and on-track performance, the team achieved the 24th position. Overall, TLMoto concluded this edition in 21st place.
